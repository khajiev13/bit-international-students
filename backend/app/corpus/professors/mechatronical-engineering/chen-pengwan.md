## Education
- Prof. Chen received his PhD. degree in engineering mechanics from Beijing Institute of Technology in 1999. Prior
- to that, he received his master and bachelor's degree from Kunming Institute of Technology, China in 1996 and
- 1993, respectively.

## Work Experience
- He worked as a postdoc researcher in Institute of Mechanics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, from 1999 to 2001. He
- joined Beijing Institute of Technology in June 2001 and received a full professor position in 2006. Now he is the
- director of school of materials science and Engineering, Beijing Institute of Technology.

## Awards
- New Century Excellent Talents in University of China, 2006
- China Education Ministry's Award for Science and Technology Progress, 2008

## Publications
- The research fields of Prof. Chen include dynamic mechanical behavior of materials, experimental techniques of
- impact dynamics, dynamic processing of materials and dynamic synthesis of materials, numerical simulation on
- impact dynamics.
