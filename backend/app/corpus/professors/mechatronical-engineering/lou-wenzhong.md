## Education
- He received his Bachelor of Engineering degree in Beijing Institute of Technology in 1990. He received his
- Master of Science in Beijing Institute of Technology in 1996, and received his PhD degree from Beijing
- Institute of Technology in Mechatronics Engineering in 2001.

## Work Experience
- Dr. Lou Wenzhong (1969- ) is professor of Mechatronics Engineering Department at Beijing Institute of
- Technology and scientist of National Key Laboratory of Mechatronics Engineering and Control.
- He worked in Xi’ an Information Research Institute from 1990 to 1993.
- He lectured 10 more courses, supervised 27 Master students and 2 PhD students since 1996. He led the group
- and received Excellent Award in China MEMS/NEMS integration competition in year 2007.

## Awards
- Editor of Journal of Automation.
- Expert for evaluation of National high-tech 863 program.
- Advanced member of Chinese MEMS/NEMS society.
- Senior director of Chinese society of micro-nano technology.
- Third prize winner of Award of National Defense Science and Technology.

## Publications
- Dr. Lou Wenzhong has received four patents, and 11 pending national patents. Dr. Lou Wenzhong also
- authored Fundamentals of MEMS Integration and Packaging Technology published in 2007 in chinese,
- Simulation Technology of MF Near Field Characteristic in 2008. He also translated Packaging
- Technology-Micro Fabrication, Packaging and Automation and published in 2008 in Chinese. Dr. Lou
- Wenzhong also published 80+ scientific papers, including 20 papers that is SCI or El indexed papers, as well
- as 30+ papers that is indexed by Chinese Scientific Core Journal.
- In the International collaboration, Dr. Lou Wenzhong joined Technical University Berlin and Fraunhofer IZM
- in German as an exchanged scholar from 2003 to 2004, focusing on advanced MEMS technology research.
