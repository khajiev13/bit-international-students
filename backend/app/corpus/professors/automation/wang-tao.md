## 研究方向(Research Interests)
1. Advanced motion control using pneumatics
2. Non-destructive testing with ultrasonic and graphic
3. Biomimetic robots based pneumatic actuator
4. Structure health monitoring

## 教育背景(Educational Background)
- Dr. Tao Wang received his Doctor degree in Mechano-Micro Engineering from the Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan in 2006.
- Prior that, he received master and bacherlor's degree from Beijing Institute of Technology, China in 1999 and 1993, respectively.

## 工作经历(Working Experience)
- Dr. Wang worked as a lecturer at the Department of Automatic Control of Beijing Institute of Technology from 1993 to 2002.
- He worked as an assistant professor at the Precision and Intelligence Laboratory of Tokyo Institute of Technology from 2006 to 2007.
- Now he is working at the School of Automation of Beijing Institute of Technology as an associate professor.
