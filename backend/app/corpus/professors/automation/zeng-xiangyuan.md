## 教育背景(Educational Background)
- Dr. Xiangyuan Zeng obtained his B.S. degree from Beijing Institute of Technology, and got his Ph.D. from Tsinghua University.
- He has ever been a visiting Ph.D. student in 2011-2012 at Texas A&M University and visited Sapienza University of Rome in 2018.
- His current research interests include asteroid exploration and defense, meteoroid dynamics and spacecraft dynamics.
- He published a few scientific papers in journals like AIAA JGCD/IEEE TAES/Icarus etc., and authored a monograph and a textbook entitled "Optimal control".
- Currently, he serves as an associate editor for the journal Advances in Space Research and the journal Astrodynamics.
- He is a member of the AIAA technical committee.
- He was elected to be one of the National level Distinguished Young Scholars and a sponsor of the Innovative Teaching and Research Studio in Beijing Area.
- Welcome students in majors of Mechanics/Aerospace Engineering/Mechanical Engineering/Automation to work together towards dynamics and control problems.
