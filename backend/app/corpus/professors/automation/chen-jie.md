## 研究方向(Research Interests)
1. Intelligent Control
2. Complex System Multi-objective Modeling and Optimization.

## 教育背景(Educational Background)
- Dr. Chen received the B.S. degree, the M.S. degree and the PHD degree in Control Theory and Control Engineering in 1986, 1993 and 2000, respectively, from the Beijing Institute of Technology.
- During 1989 through 1990, he was a visiting scholar in California State University, U.S.A.
- From 1996 to 1997, he was a Research Fellow in School of E&E, the university of Birmingham, U.K.

## 工作经历(Working Experience)
- He is currently the Assistant President of Beijing Institute of Technology, and Director of the Office of Research and Development Administration, Beijing Institute of Technology.
