## 教育背景(Educational Background)
- Dong Lei was born in China, 1967.
- He received the B.S. degree from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, China, in 1990, and the Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, China, in 2000.
- He worked as a Postdoctoral in Tsinghua University China, from 2000 to 2002.

## 工作经历(Working Experience)
- He joined AVIC The First Aircraft Institute as an Engineer from 1990 to 1995. During his five years in AVIC The First Aircraft Institute, he was the project leader of the power electronics group.
- He developed a number of electrical techniques for FBC-1 and Y7-200A aircrafts power system, environmental control system, lighting system.
- He is also the project leader of National Natural Science Foundation of China of "modeling and prediction of wind power generation in large wind farms".
- He has published over 50 papers and 3 books. He is now the associate professor in Beijing Institute of Technology, China and director of Power Electronics and Motion Control Research Centre of the university. His research interests include power electronics and motion control, micro grid, and renewable energy.
