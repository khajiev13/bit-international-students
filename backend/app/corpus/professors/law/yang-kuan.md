## Biography
- 杨宽，汉族，中共党员，法学博士、博士后，北京理工大学法学院长聘副教授、博士生导师、院长助理，北京理工大学空天政策与法律研究院副院长。
- Dr. Kuan Yang is an Associate Professor and PhD supervisor of international law at the School of Law of Beijing Institute of Technology. He is also the Assistant Dean of the BIT Law School, the Deputy Dean of the BIT Academy of Air and Space Policy and Law, Member of Board (Directors) of the Chinese Society of International Law, Member of Board (Directors) of the China Institute of Space Law, Member of Board (Directors) and Secretary-General of the Beijing Society of International Law, and researcher at the Space Law Center of China National Space Administration (CNSA).
- His research interests lie in public international law, international space law, international air law, and the law of the WTO. He has published in several fields of international law and worked on research projects funded by SASTIND, CNSA, APSCO, the China Institute of Space Law, etc.
