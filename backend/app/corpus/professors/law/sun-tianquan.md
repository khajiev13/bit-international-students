## Research Interests

- 公司法 / Company Law

## Education
- 1992-1996: 吉林大学法学院本科 / B.A. in law from Law School, Jilin University.
- 1996-1999: 吉林大学法学院民商法学硕士 / LL.M. in civil and commercial law from Law School, Jilin University.
- 2000-2003: 吉林大学法学院法理学博士 / Ph.D. in jurisprudence from Law School, Jilin University.

## Work Experience
- 1998: 中国律师资格考试合格 / Qualification in the Chinese Bar Examination.
- 2010: 北京理工大学副教授 / Professional title for an associate professor.
- 2003年至今: 北京理工大学讲师、副教授 / From 2003, lecturer and later associate professor in BIT.
