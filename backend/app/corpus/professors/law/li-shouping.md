## Basic Information
- Name: 李寿平
- Pinyin: Li Shouping
- Title: 教授 / Professor
- School: 法学院 / School of Law
- Email: lishouping@bit.edu.cn

## Research Interests
- 国际公法 / Public international law
- 国际空间法 / International space law
- 国际航空安保法 / Aviation safety
- 国际组织法 / International institution law

## Education
- 2001-2003: 博士后，复旦大学 / Post-doctor, Fudan University.
- 2001: 博士，武汉大学法学院 / PhD, Wuhan University.

## Work Experience
- 国际宇航科学院社会科学学部通讯院士 / Academician of International Academy of Astronautics.
- 北京理工大学法学院院长 / Dean of the School of Law of Beijing Institute of Technology.
- 北京理工大学空间法研究所主任 / Director of Space Law Institute of BIT.

## Academic Service and Memberships
- 中国国际法学会理事 / Member of Board (directors) of China Institute of International Law.
- 中国法学会法学教育研究会理事 / Member of Board (directors) of China association of Legal Education.
- 中国空间法学会常务理事 / Executive Member of Board (directors) of China Institute of Space Law.
- 北京国际法学会常务理事兼秘书长 / General Secretary of Beijing Society of International Law.
- 中国欧洲学会欧盟法研究会副会长 / Vice President of China Association of EU Law.

## Awards

- 2011年入选北京市社会科学理论人才“百人工程” / One of “one hundred Beijing social science theory talents” in 2011.
