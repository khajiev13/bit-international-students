## Biography
- Graduated from Heilongjiang University, School of Law, with Master's degree of civil law in 1997; since then worked in Beijing Institute of Technology.
- 李艳秋副教授任职于北京理工大学法学院，主要研究方向是商法学，承担商法专题研究、商法判例研习法等研究生课程的教学工作。发表商法学、法学基本理论等方面的论文多篇，多次参与法学各类教材与专著的撰写工作。从事教学。
- Take on special topic research and teaching of commercial law, published lots of books and papers about commercial law and law basic theory etc.
