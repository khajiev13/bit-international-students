## Basic Information
- Name: 郭德忠
- Pinyin: Guo Dezhong
- Title: 副教授 / Associate Professor
- School: 法学院 / School of Law
- Email: guodezhong@163.com

## Research Interests
- 知识产权法 / Intellectual property law
- 竞争法 / Competition law
- 科技法研究 / Science and technology law

## Education
- 1991: 北京理工大学电子工程系微电子电路与系统专业工学学士 / Bachelor of Engineering, Beijing Institute of Technology.
- 2001: 中国政法大学法律硕士 / Juris Master, China University of Political Science and Law.
- 2005: 北京大学法学院法学博士 / PhD of Civil and Commercial Law, Peking University.
- 2010: 中南财经政法大学博士后 / Post Doctorate of Law, Zhongnan University of Economic and Law.

## Work Experience
- 2016: 北京理工大学法学院副院长 / Deputy Dean of School of Law, Beijing Institute of Technology.
- 2005-至今: 北京理工大学讲师、副教授 / 2005-current: Associate Professor, Beijing Institute of Technology.

## Academic Service and Memberships
- 中国知识产权法学研究会理事 / Director of Chinese Intellectual Property Law Association.
- 国家知识产权战略专家库成员 / Expert Panel Member of National Intellectual Property Strategy.
- 商务部企业知识产权海外维权援助中心专家库成员 / Expert Panel Member of Enterprise Intellectual Property Rights Overseas Aid Center of Ministry of Commerce.
- 北京市法学会科技法学研究会理事 / Director of Science and Technology Law Association of Law Institute of Beijing.
- 北京市法学会知识产权法研究会理事 / Director of Intellectual Property Law Association of Law Institute of Beijing.
- 北京市法学会影视娱乐法研究会理事 / Director of Film and Television Entertainment Law Association of Law Institute of Beijing.
