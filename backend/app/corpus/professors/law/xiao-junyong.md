## Research Interests

## Education
- 1997-2000: Law School of Hunan Normal University, Major of Jurisprudence, LLM.
- 2001-2004: Graduate School of Chinese Academy of Social Science, Major of Jurisprudence, PhD.

## Work Experience
- 1994-1997: Office of Research and Graduate, Hunan College of Financial and Economics, Secretary and editor.
- 2000-2004: Lecture of Law School of Hunan University.
- 2004-2019: Associate Professor and Professor of Law Dept of University of International Relations (UIR, Beijing).
- 2007-2008: Guest Researcher of Georgetown University Law Center in USA.
- 2015-2016: Visiting Professor of Law School of Hamburg University in Germany.
- 2020-: Professor of Law School of Beijing Institute of Technology.

## Academic Service and Memberships
- Member of the China Association of Constitutional Law Research.
- Member of the China Society for Human Rights Studies.
- Member of Legislative Research Association.
- Member of National Security Law Research Association.

## Awards
- National advanced individual in law popularization during the 5th Five Year Plan period (2011).
- Excellent professional course lecturer in Beijing universities (2019); others.

## Publications
