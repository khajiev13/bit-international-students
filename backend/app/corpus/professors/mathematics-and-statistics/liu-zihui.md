## Basic Information
- Name: 刘子辉
- Pinyin: Liu Zihui
- Title: 副教授 / Associate Professor
- School: 数学与统计学院 / School of Mathematics and Statistics
- Discipline: 编码理论 / Coding Theory
- Email: lzhui@bit.edu.cn

## Research Interests
- Finite Fields（有限域）
- Coding Theory（编码理论）
- Finite Geometry（有限几何）

## Education
- Ph.D: Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences（中国科学院数学与系统科学研究院获得博士学位）
- Master Degree: East China Normal University（华东师范大学获得学士和硕士学位）

## Work Experience
- Finished Post Doctor research work in 2005, then joined the Department of Mathematics, Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT). At present, Associate Professor in BIT.
- 2005年北京大学博士后出站后, 加入北京理工大学数学与统计学院, 目前是北京理工大学数学与统计学院副教授。

## Academic Service and Memberships
- Reviewer of Mathematics Review
- Reviewer of many international journals such as IEEE-IT, Designs Codes and Cryptography, Information Sciences, etc.
