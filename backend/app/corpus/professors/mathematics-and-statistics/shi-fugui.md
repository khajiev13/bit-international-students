## Research Interests
- Fuzzy topological spaces
- Fuzzy matroids
- Fuzzy convex spaces

## Education
- Fu-Gui Shi was born in Shuangyashan, Heilongjiang, China, in 1962.
- He received the B.S. degree from Mudanjiang Teachers College, Heilongjiang, and the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Capital Normal University, Beijing, China, in 1985, 1988, and 2001, respectively.

## Work Experience
- From 1987 to 1993, he worked as a teacher at Mudanjiang Teachers College.
- From 1993 to 1998, he served as a Professor at Yantai Normal University, Shandong, China.
- Since 2001, he has been working as a Professor at Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing, China.

## Academic Service and Memberships
- Member of the Advisory Board of Iranian Journal of Fuzzy Systems (IJFS)
- Member of Editorial Committee of the Journal of Fuzzy Systems and Mathematics, Asian Journal of Fuzzy and Applied Mathematics, Research in Applied Mathematics, International Scholarly Research Notices, Journal of Advanced Studies in Topology, and so on.
