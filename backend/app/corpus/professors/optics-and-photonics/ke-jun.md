- Imaging Technology ——
- Dr. Ke's research interests are in the area of computational imaging especially compressive sensing. She has
- extensive research experience on compressive sensing and compressive passive and active optical imaging,
- signal and image processing technology such as linear/nonlinear reconstruction, data compression, and source
- separation. She has also done research works on biomedical imaging projects including digital holography
- and optical coherent tomography (OCT), computational photography, and statistical estimation & detection
- theory, pattern/face recognition. Dr. Ke's current research focus is on the applications of compressive imaging
- in areas such as free space optical communication, night vision, etc. She also works on projects in
- optoelectronic detection. Her research results have been published in Applied Optics, Optics Express,
- Biomedical Optics Express, and Optics Communications, as well as various international conferences.

## Education
- 教育背景: (Educational Background)
- Dr Jun Ke received her PhD. degree in Electrical & Computer Engineering from the University of Arizona,
- USA in 2010. Prior to that, she received her bachelor degree and her first master degree both from the
- University of Electronic and Science Technology of China (UESTC) in 1996 and 1999, respectively. She had
- her second master degree from Purdue University, USA in 2002.

## Work Experience
