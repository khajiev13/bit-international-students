- Dr. Zhao's research interests are in the area of optical precision measurement and differential confocal. He has
- extensive research experience on the integrating and modeling of instruments. Dr. Zhao's current research|
- focus is on the optical parameters measurement of optical system and elements using the differential confocal
- focusing technique and differential confocal microscopy. His research results have been published in Optics
- Express, Measurement and Science Technology, Optics Communications, Applied Optics, Review of|
- Instrument Science, Optik, as well as some international conferences.
- He preside the items of Major National scientific instrument and equipment development project of china,
- and National 863 major project.

## Education
- SFR: (Educational Background)
- Dr. Weigian Zhao received his Ph.D. degree in Instrument Science and Technology from the Harbin Institute
- of Technology, China in 2005. Prior to that, he received his master and bachelor's degree from Harbin
- University of Science and Technology and Harbin Institute of Technology. China in 1988 and 1993,
- respectively.

## Work Experience
- 工作经历: (Working Experience)
- Dr. Zhao worked as an assistant professor at the Automation and Control Department of Harbin Institute of
- Technology from 1997 to 2002, and he worked as a professor at the Automation and Control Department of
- Harbin Institute of Technology from 2002 to 2006. He joined the school of Optoelectronics of Beijing
- Institute of Technology as a professor.
