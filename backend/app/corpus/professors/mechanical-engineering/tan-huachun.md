## Research and Teaching Interest
- Our research is focused on technologies of internet of vehicles,high-dimensional multisource big data analysis. We proposed a tensor model to represent traffic data and study the tensor based traffic data analysis methods. The high-dimensional multisource traffic data analysis is an important part of study on the technologies of internet of vehicles. And it can be expanded to big data analysis for other fields.
