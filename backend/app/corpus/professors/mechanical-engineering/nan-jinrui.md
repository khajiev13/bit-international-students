## Research and Teaching Interest
- Research areas include automotive bus,car electronics,new energy vehicle control technology and energy management technology. With team members in the country carried out the first electric vehicle controller,battery management and electric air conditioning system design frontier research. Successfully applied science and technology research results Olympic Games,World Expo and the Asian Games,and together with team members Ministry of Education award. Also studied electronically controlled air suspension control technology cutting-edge technology,and successfully obtained the enterprise application and promotion.
