## Research and Teaching Interest
- Our research is focused on the Intelligent Transportation Systems,Vehicle Smart and Modern Logistics. Evaluation of human exposure to whole-body vibration,human-vehicle- environment interaction,monitoring and evaluating of drivers,vehicle smart and unmanned driving vehicle test,optimization of modern logistics systems and intelligent computation are developed. Moreover,human evaluation and unmanned driving vehicle test evaluation take first level place at home. Higher level conclusions on evaluation of human exposure to whole-body vibration at lying posture,comprehensive evaluation of drivers and VRP intelligent computation have been made. First driver comprehensive evaluation data base and information management system of commercial vehicles was established in China.
