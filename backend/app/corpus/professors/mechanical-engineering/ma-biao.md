## Research and Teaching Interest
- Focused on vehicle transmission theory and technology,lubrication and contamination control for transmissions,and vehicle fault diagnosis techniques. My research team focus on the design and development of hydro-mechanical integrated transmissions and multi-gear mechanical transmissions. We contribute to the development of complete theory,design and experimental test of integrated transmissions for light tracked vehicles. Our achievements are of great benefit to the manufacture of light integrated transmissions and the evolution of transmission technology.
