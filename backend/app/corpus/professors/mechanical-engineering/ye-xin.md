## Research and Teaching Interest
- Associate Professor Ye’s current research interests are in process and equipment technology of precision assembly,micro manufacturing technology,detection technology on assembly process. She has undertaken or take part in over 20s research projects,such as the National Natural Science Foundations,key equipment pre-research projects,Key projects of National Defense Basic Research,National Science and Technology Major Projects. She has published more than 30 papers,indexed by SCI or EI,in academic journals and conferences; has taken part in editing 2 books,such as Mechanical Control Engineering Fundamentals,Precision micro Turn-milling Technology. Associate Profofessor Ye is a recipient of over 20 national invention patents. The innovation and academic level of a number of research results are given a high evaluation in domestic counterparts,and have been applied in a number of aerospace,weapons and other fields.
