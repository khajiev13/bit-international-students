## Research and Teaching Interest
- 1. Thermal aerodynamics of turbomachinery,Turbocharging technology,design and optimization as well as flow control in axial/radial flow compressors and turbines. 2. Research covers flow-thermal,flow-structure issues,using CFD-based technology for turbomachine design and optimization on gas turbine,fell cell compressors,vehicular engine turbocharger,off-road turbocharging system and marine power turbochargers.
