## Research and Teaching Interest
- My research is focused on the Reconfigurable Manufacturing System and Product Design based on Knowledge. In the field of Reconfigurable Manufacturing System,I work on the optimal design of reconfiguration,the decision making for the reconfigurable timing,the evaluation for the schema of RMS,and the modeling and simulation. For the Product Design based on Knowledge,I work on the modeling of knowledge,product process modeling and design decision making.
