## Research and Teaching Interest
- Our research is focused on the transmission design，including multi-DOF planetary mechanism，gear mechanism，hybrid transmission,and power split transmission. Topological model building and analysis is my research field. Vibration of high speed wet clutch and drag torque model for high density power transmission are other research field. The model prediction and countermeasure are successfully used in high speed hybrid tracked vehicle.
