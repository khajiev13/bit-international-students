## Research and Teaching Interest
- Research interests on Mechanical Machine and Ultrasonic Nondestructive Testing. The researches include the Theory and Method of Nondestructive Testing for 3D Residual Stress Profile by Ultrasonic Wave which is supported by National Natural Science Foundation of China,the Mechanism of Nondestructive Testing Residual Stress in Metal Component which is supported by Research Fund for the Doctoral Program of Higher Education of China,Research on Residual Stress Profile Testing by Ultrasonic which is supported by Beijing Higher Education Young Elite Teacher Project.
