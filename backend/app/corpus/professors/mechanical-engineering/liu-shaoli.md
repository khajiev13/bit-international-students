## Research and Teaching Interest
- Dr. Liu's research is in the areas of machine vision and on-line detection. She is interested in the image processing and 3D reconstruction for machine vision-based detection. Specifically,she is studying the calibration of cameras,point clouds processing,3D reconstruction for precision detection.
