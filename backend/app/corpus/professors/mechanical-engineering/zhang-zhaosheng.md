## Research and Teaching Interest
- Our research is focused on the data analysis of connected vehicle,especially the impact of the vehicle condition and road shape on vehicle moving speed to establish dynamic path planning model under connected vehicle networking. Studies of the impact of vehicle travelling environment and traffic characteristics on time-consumption of vehicle running under vehicle networking environment can help set up Theoretical Evaluation Model of Path Impedance.
