- Mainly focus on teaching and research work of Product Lifecycle Manufacturing Services and Product Service System. Has presided over 1 National Natural Science Fund project,2 National High Technology Research and Development Planning(863 Planning) projects. 4 national and enterprise cooperation projects are in research.
