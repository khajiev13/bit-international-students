## Research and Teaching Interest
- My research is focused on the vehicle intelligent gearshift control theory,vehicle automatic transmission technology,vehicle dynamics control,etc. As the project leader,I finished the project called “The development of the wheel type vehicle’s automatic gear shifting control system”,which took three years,and finished road test in various kinds of complex environment like plateau,cold regions,and cross-country. The total mileage of the road test is 40,000 km and it makes a great contribution to domestic AMT technologies in China. As the project leader,I finished the project called “The development of automatic transmission in the second generation SUV”,which was funded by Beijing Municipal Science and Technology Commission. This is the first SUV,which use electronic-controlled electronic-powered AMT technology in our country. Also,Funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China,I do researches on vehicle intelligent shift theory and key technologies. This research is to improve the adaption of the automatic transmission system to different driving environments (slope,turn situation and low friction road) and realize the intelligent control in these specific driving environments.
