## Research and Teaching Interest
- Our research is focused on fields of hydrodynamic transmission technology,vehicular transmission technology and vehicle modern design method. We are conducting the advanced research works on Integrated design and manufacure techniques of torque converter,multi-funtion hydrodyanmic torque converter and retarders,vehicle driveline realtime simulation techniques based on virtual assmbly,high power density turbomachiery flow field test and CFD analysis. Hydrodynamic transmission research area are of the leading positon in domestic level,and three dimensional flow design of hydrodynamic components are of the leading position in international level and series products are developed. The relevant achievements are applied to several national projects and pre-study projects,as won the state level prize once,the ministerial level prize five times.
