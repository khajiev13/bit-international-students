## Research and Teaching Interest
- Our researches are mainly focused on electric vehicle theory,technology,and application. Our research content mainly includes nonlinear parameter identification and management of electric vehicle battery system,charging infrastructure planning theory and system matching method,battery switching technologies,group technologies of battery,and battery thermal runaway theory. We first globally developed a rapid battery replacement device of electric coach,which has been successfully used in Beijing Olympic Games. Besides,in the field of battery system and charging infrastructure,our research results have been successfully used in Beijing Olympic Games,Shanghai World Expo and Guangzhou Asian Games. In addition,we first established the norms of electric vehicle charging infrastructure,included 19 norms for Beijing,and we placed second in the Beijing Science and Technology Progress Award. Moreover,we presided over the construction and development of Beijing electric vehicle safety monitoring and management platform,which can realize synchronized monitoring of over 100,000 vehicle data. Furthermore,we are managing the largest electric vehicle applications network of public domain.
