## Research and Teaching Interest
- Yue Ma is an associate professor of Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT),Beijing,China. He obtained his MSc degree in vehicle engineering from BIT in 2002. Then,he has been working as a staff in School of Vehicular and Mechanical Engineering of BIT. In 2010,he obtained his PhD degree at Bristol Robotics Laboratory in University of the West of England,UK. His main research interest is in the area of ground vehicle system dynamics and control,especially in unmanned vehicle dynamics,electrical-mechanical transmission control.
