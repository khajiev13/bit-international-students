## Research and Teaching Interest
- Integration design,controller development,simulation and experimental evaluation of power-train in electric vehicles including battery electric vehicle,hybrid electric vehicle and fuel cell electric vehicle; Control strategy and systematic optimization of power-train in hybrid electric vehicles; Research and development on battery management system; Algorithm study on SOC/SOH of batteries and available range in electric vehicles; Development of information monitor and fault diagnosis system in electric vehicles.
