## Research and Teaching Interest
- The research interest includes precision and ultra-precision grinding,cutting of difficult-to-cut materials,design and fabrication of micro-tools,and machine tools. The innovation researches including three dimensional spiral ultrasonic assisted grinding of hard and brittle material,ultrasonic vibration-ultrasonic cavitation assisted drilling and pulsed magnetic treatment method of micro-tools,have been carried out firstly in domestic. And the state invention patents about the above research have been declared as well. Furthermore,the developed CNC torsional axis machine tool of root reinforcement has been successfully used in a key enterprises.
