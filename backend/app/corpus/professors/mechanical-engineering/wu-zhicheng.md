## Research and Teaching Interest
- Our research is focused on automotive dynamics,automotive design technology,automotive chassis control technology,etc. The design work has been involved to complete the design of two type vehicles,parachute assault vehicles and airborne tractor. At present,the main research direction is to analyze and optimize the overall performance of the vehicle by the vehicle virtual prototype and kinematics / dynamics simulation technology. Research and development of controllable suspension system for vehicle height adjustment and damping control. Research and development of advanced steering system for improving vehicle handling and stability.
