## Research and Teaching Interest
- Electric vehicle motor drive technology and power electronics in electric vehicle application technology are included in research. The team firstly developed a power drive system for electric buses with permanent magnet synchronous motor in China. And trended to realize a series of product application by cooperating with enterprises in domestic. The related technology has been won the state invention the second award.
