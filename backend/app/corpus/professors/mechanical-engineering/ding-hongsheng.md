## Research and Teaching Interest
- Teaching is always my responsibility,particularly on mechanical design and theory,as well as for research and discipline construction. Our research is focused on mechanism and robotics mechanics,engineering practice education,etc. It is my responsibility to lead several municipal and national projects of educational reform,and for BIT. As the main editor and co-editor,there are more than 10 books as teaching material. There are over 80 scientific papers of mine by far. I am named as the distinguished teacher in Beijing,and is awarded as the Patriotic meritorious model in Beijing. I am the excellent worker for Beijing Higher Education for Self-Directed Learners. My successes achieved in teaching won the first prize of 2 items,and the second prize of 3 items in Beijing Higher Education Competition. As the director of the Engineering Training Center,the center is named as the National Electronic Experimental Education Demonstration Center. The team of the Engineering Training Center is named as the National Level Teaching Group,and the course of “Basic Training of Manufacture” is rated as the superfine courses in Beijing.
