## Research and Teaching Interest
- My research is focused on the design method and theory of vehicle transmission and NVH technology of chassis. We have done some research works on vehicle dynamics,noise and vibration control and electrical and mechanical compound transmission. Our research results on nonlinear system dynamics and electrical and mechanical drive technology have been used in many national projects and awarded a second prize of Ministry Science and Technology Progress.
