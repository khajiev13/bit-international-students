## Research and Teaching Interest
- Research is focused on dynamic systems and control and its application in new energy vehicle system simulation,optimization and vehicle dynamics control. Assume a number projects of National Natural Science Fund Committee,the National Defense Basic Research,National High-tech R&D Program (863 Program),the Beijing Science and Technology Commission and the automotive industry cooperation. By a number of provincial and ministerial level scientific research award,selected the outstanding young teachers of Beijing Institute of Technology program,published more than 40 academic papers,3 national invention patents. Deputy editor of IEEE transportation electrification,Member of Ministry of Industry and Information Technology APEC automotive working group,IEEE senior member,member of vehicle control board IFAC,senior member of China Institute of Mechanical Engineering,National Natural Science Fund Committee letter experts.
