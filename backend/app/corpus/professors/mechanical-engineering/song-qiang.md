## Research and Teaching Interest
- Our research is focused on the technology of design,control,optimization,and test experiment of electric vehicle’s energy management,driving motor,and powertrain system. The research field includes test method of driving motor system,performance test and evaluation of key materials and parts of motor system,transmission optimization,and also the control strategy of powertrain on electric bulldozer.
