## Research and Teaching Interest
- The research includes Electric vehicle energy management and control,Vehicle energy storage technology,etc. Moreover,the research also focuses on Vehicle electric drive system integration design,Battery management system design and Battery thermal management system. The design of battery management system has successfully used in a variety of hybrid electric vehicles in the military field.
