## Education
- （Educational Background）：Dr. CHEN Qi holds both his B.S. and M.S. degrees from Tsinghua University, under the guidance of Prof. SHI Gaoquan. He received his Ph.D. degree at University of California, Los Angeles （UCLA）. His advisor was Prof. PEI Qibing.

## Work Experience
- （Working Experience）：From 2013 to 2016, he worked as a postdoc fellow at California Nanosystem Institute （CNSI）， UCLA, with Prof. YANG Yang's supervision.
