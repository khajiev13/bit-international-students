## Education
- （Educational Background） He was born in Yunyang, Sichuan Province of China in June, 1951. He joined the army in April, 1968.From
- 1968 to 1979, he was a firearms repairman and assistant engineer at ordnance station in Duyun Military Subarea in Guizhou. He received the Bachelors degree in chemistry from Guizhou Normal University （Guiyang Teachers college） in 1982. In 1992, he studied for doctors degree at National University of Defense Technology.

## Work Experience
- （Working Experience） Prof. Liu Jiping is a scientist in energetic materials and flame retardant materials. He is currently a full professor in the School of Materials Science and Engineering, Beijing Institute of Technology, China While he was in Cadre Culture School in Guizhou Military Command from 1982 to 1985, he worked as Lab assistant, teacher and statf officer. And from 1985 to 1995, he worked as a project group head, engineer， senior engineer and salary advanced senior engineer successively at Research Section 1, Air Force Rescarch Institute of Aerial Ammunition. He has enjoyed the government special allowance form the State Council since 1992 and was granted"outstanding voung scientific expert' from the Ministry of Personnel, in 1992.
- [Source page 2]
- He was brought in the School of Chemical and Materials, Beijing Institute of Technology（BIT） in 1995, and from then on he has been researcher, professor, administrative vice director and vice director of National Laboratory of Flame Retardant Materials, etc. He was awarded as trans-century talent by Ministry of Education in 1996. He has wotked in various fields of materials science and technology in application as well as in basic research, and has made many great achievements in scientific research, which have created good political， martial and social benefits.

## Awards
- （Awards） “Outstanding Young Scientific Expert from the Ministry of Personnel, in 1992； Trans-Century Talent by Ministry of Education Award in1996； HLHL Advancement Prize in 2005：Two National Prizes （the second） for invention； Two first provincial prizes for science and technology progress：Four provincial prizes for great contribution and， Five other prizes for science and technology as the first completed person.

## Publications
- （Publications） His research and achievements were focused on the tields of energetic materials, flame retardant materials， new type energy sources materials and special materials, ete. He has been awarded the second national prizes two times for invention, two first provincial prizes for science and technology progress, four provincial prizes for great contribution and five other prizes for science and technology as the first completed person. And He was the HLHL Advancement Prize Winner 2005. He has got fifteen inventive patents and has authored and coauthored over 260 papers, one collection of papers and five books. He has trained a lot of research persons with abilities in energetic materials, flame retardant materials and nanomaterials, and promoted our national materials research greatly. He is a part time professor and doctoral tutor at Beijing University of Aeronautics & Astronautics and Sichuan University.
