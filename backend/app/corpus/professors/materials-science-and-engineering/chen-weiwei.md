## Education
- （Educational Background） Dr. Chen obtained his Bachelor's degree in Chongqing University in 2004. Then, he enrolled in the University of Science & Technology Beijing for Master study. In 2007, he went to the University of Auckland （New Zealand） as Research Assistant. During 2008 through 2010, Dr. Chen followed PhD program in the University of Auckland. He obtained "Spark Idea Award" in 2009， "New Zealand Study Abroad Award”in
