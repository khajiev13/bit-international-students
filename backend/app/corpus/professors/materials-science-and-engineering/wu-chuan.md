## Work Experience
- （Working Experience） Chuan WU is currently a professor at Beijing Institute of Technology, where he has been responsible for new energy materials, as well as devdoping green batteries based on multi-electron materials. He is experienced in electrodhemical energy conversion and storage systems （Li-ion batteries, Na-ion batteries, Al/Zn/Ca secondary batteries, alkaline batteries, etc.）. From 2002 to 2004, he worked as a postdoctoral research fellow in the fuel cell center of Dalian Institute of Chemical Physics; Chinese Academy of Sciences, focusing on the catalysts for PEMFC, DAFC, as well as the catalysts for in situ hydrogen generation.
