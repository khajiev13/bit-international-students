## Biography
- Hou-min Li received hisB.S. degree from Tsinghua University, Beijing China, in 2005, and
- Various M.S. degrees and a Ph.D. degree from University of Southem Califomia, Los Angeles, CA，
- and Aubum University: Aubum, AL, in 2006, 2008, and 2011, respectively. During his study, he also
- joined Shaw Industry Group, Dalton, GA, as a co-op engineer for a year. After he received his Ph.D.
- degree, he joined the faculty of Beijing Institute of Technology in 2011, where he developed two
- undergraduate and graduate clectromagnetics and microwave engineering courses and co-authored
- Introduction to Electromagnetics for the junior-level core course for electrical and computer
- engineering students. His research interests include advanced electromagnetic materials, low-loss
- broadband microwave and millimeter-wave circuits, and advanced manufacturing of microwave and
- millimeter-wavecomponents （3D printed circuits）.
