## Publications
- 发表文章（Publications）
- 消列学5左右代表性论文或著作。（Please list S representative publications）
- Dr. Liu's research interests are in the area of application specific instruction Set processor architecture and
- design. He has extensive research experience on low-power reconfigurable architecture for communication and
- multimedia processing, embedded system and multi-core processor design. Dr. Liu's current research focus is
- on prime-factor containing FFT implementation, which includes both hardware architectural design /modeling
- and the development of eficient algorithms. His research results have been published in Elsevier Joumal of
- procedia engineering and various international top conferences.
