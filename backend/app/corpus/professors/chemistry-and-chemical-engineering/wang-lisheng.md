## Biography
- Prof. Dr. Lisheng Wang received his PhD. degree in Chemical Engineering from the China University of Petroleum (CUP) in 1992. From 1992-1994, He was a post doctorial fellow in CUP. Since 1994, Dr. L.S. Wang worked as an assistant professor from 1994-1996 at Beijing Institute of Technology. From 1996-1997, and in 2002, He worked as a visiting Scholar at University of Oldenburg, Germany. He was a visiting Scholar at the Russia University of Chemical Technology during 2000. He worked as a Professor at Beijing Institute of Technology from 1996-2012. Dr. Wang's research interests are in the area of equation of state, chemical engineering thermodynamics and fluid phase equilibrium, ionic liquids and green chemical process. His research results have been published in AIChE Joumal, Chem. Eng. Sci, Journal of Chemical Engineering Data, Ind. & Eng. Res., Fluid Phase Equilibria, Green Chemistry, Applied Catalysis A, as well as various intemational conferences.
