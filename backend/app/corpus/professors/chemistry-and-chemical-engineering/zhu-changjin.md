## Biography
- His research interests have been the areas of medicinal and material chemistry. His current research focus is on the design and synthesis of aldose reductase inhibitors and organic polymers for functional membranes. He has published papers in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry, ChemMedChem, European Journal of Medicinal Chemistry, Bioorganic and Medicinal Chemistry, Journal of Natural Products, Current Medicinal Chemistry. He has received research grants from the National Natural Science Foundation of China, the Research Fund for the Doctoral Program of Higher Education of China, and the Beijing Natural Science Foundation, China.
