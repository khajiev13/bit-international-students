## Biography
- Dr. Jiao's research interests are mainly on Functional nanomaterials and Polymer/inorganic nanocomposites. He is engaged in the synthesis, structure, properties and application of graphene, carbon nanotubes, TiO2, ferrites and layered double hydroxides with different nanostructures. Right now his interest lies in the functionalization of carbon nanotubes and properties of graphene/ferrite nanocomposites. In addition, his research is focused on the polymer/nanomaterial composites including PI/CNT nanocomposites, ABS/CNT/ferrite composites, PET/LDH nanocomposites. He has published more than 100 papers and patents in these areas.
