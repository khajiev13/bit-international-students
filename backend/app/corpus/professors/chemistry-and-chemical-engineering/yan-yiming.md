## Biography
- Prof. Yi-Ming Yen received his PhD in 2006 at the Institute of Chemistry, CAS. Since 2006 he had been working with Prof. Willner at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Israel, for Biofuel cell research for two years. Then, he received the Humboldt Fellowship and worked as Postdoc for two years in IMTEK of Freiburg University, Germany. In 2008, he worked together with Prof. Lo Gorton as a short-term Humboldt visiting scholar in Lund University, Sweden. He is currently a professor in the Beijing Institute of Technology. His research interests focus on Electrochemistry, New Energy materials and technologies, and Analytical Chemistry.
