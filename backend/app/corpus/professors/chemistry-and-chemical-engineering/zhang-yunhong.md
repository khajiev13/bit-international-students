## Biography
- Dr. Zhang's research interests are in the area of atmospheric chemistry and physics. He has extensive research experience on thermodynamic and dynamic research of aerosols, including Raman spectroscopy and FTIR spectroscopy observation on hygroscopic properties of inorganic and organic aerosols, reaction of organic acid with 03, optical tweezers applied on monitoring evaporation dynamic process of single droplets. His research results have been published in JPC, PCCP, JCP.AC and other journals.
