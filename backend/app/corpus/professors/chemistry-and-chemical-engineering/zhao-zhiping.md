## Biography
- Prof. Zhao's research interests are in the area of membrane science and technology and biomass energy. He has extensive research experience on membrane material design and preparation, membrane formation, modeling and simulation of membrane separation processes, and bioconversion and chemical conversion of biomasses. Prof. Zhao's current research focus is on membrane modification by plasma and UV grafting for catalysis, antimicrobial, and hydrophility properties, membrane distillation for desalination and concentration, and membrane bioreactor for water purification. His research results have been published in Journal of Membrane Science, Industrial and Engineering Chemistry Research, Desalination, Plasma Chemistry and Plasma Processes, as well as various international conferences.
