## Biography
- Prof. Dr. Chen's research interests are in the area of Asymmetric Catalysis and synthesis of aza-heterocyclic compounds. He has extensive research experience on asymmetric Lewis-Acid catalysis, asymmetric organocatalysis, chiral anion catalysis. and asymmetric transition metal catalysis of organometallic chemistry. Prof. Dr. Chen's current research focus is on asymmetric synthesis of CF3-containing compounds, asymmetric synthesis of nitrioles, and synthesis of 1,2,4-oxadiazoles. His research results have been published in Organic Letters, Chemistry_A European Journal, Journal of Organic Chemistry, and other international journals.
