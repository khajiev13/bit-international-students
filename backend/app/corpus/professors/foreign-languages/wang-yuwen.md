## Basic Information
- Name: 王玉雯
- Pinyin: Wang Yuwen
- Title: 讲师 / Lecturer
- School: 外国语学院 / The School of Foreign Language
- Email: wyw@bit.edu.cn

## Research Interests
- 应用语言学 / Applied Linguistics
- 计算机辅助外语教学 / Computer Aided Language Teaching

## Education
- 2007年毕业于英国西英格兰大学人文与社会科学学院，获得博士学位。/ Obtained Ph.D. in 2007 awarded by the University of the West of England, U.K.
- 1997年9月至1998年9月在英国兰卡斯特大学语言学系做访问学者，主修课堂教学和语言测试。/ Studied classroom teaching and language testing at the University of Lancaster during Sept. 1997-Sept. 1998 as an academic visitor sponsored by China Scholarship Council.

## Work Experience
- 主讲硕士生专业英语学术论文写作，非英语专业硕士生科技英语论文写作和硕士生公共英语。
- I began to work in Beijing Institute of Technology in 1989 and am currently working as a professor in the School of Foreign Languages, teaching academic writing to English major graduate students and scientific writing to non-English major graduate students.
- 主持的“网络辅助公共英语教学与硕士生英语应用能力的培养”课题于2005年9月获北京市教育教学成果二等奖，2006年北京市优秀教师。主编的《新世纪研究生英语系列教程》第二版获北京市精品教材。
- 主持过国家留学基金委、中国学位与研究生教育学会“十一五”和“十二五”、北京十方元基科技有限公司研究课题以及北京理工大学硕士生英语教改课题等。目前负责中国学位与研究生教育学会“十二五”教研课题一项。
- I worked as the head of different research projects sponsored by China Scholarship Council, China Academic Degrees and Graduate Education Association, Beijing Shifang Yuanji Science and Technology Limited Company and Beijing Institute of Technology.

## Awards

- Chief-editor, “Excellent Coursebooks: Integrated Course; Listen, Speak and Watch,” awarded by The Beijing Municipal Commission of Education.
- “Outstanding Teacher” awarded by The Beijing Municipal Commission of Education.
