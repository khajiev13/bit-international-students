## Work Experience
- 1997.7至今: 北京理工大学外国语学院。
- 主要讲授非英语专业研究生学术英语、综合英语、英语视听说等。共发表论文13篇，其中CSSCI 5篇，参与编写各种教材16本，共计约150万字。目前在研项目2项，已完成课题3项，参与教研室研究课题8项。
- 2005.7-2005.8: 美国伊利诺伊大学培训 / Illinois Institute of Technology Training.
- 2010.9-2011.8: 剑桥大学英语系英语及应用语言学中心访学 / Research Centre for English and Applied Linguistics, University of Cambridge, Academic Visitor.
- 1997.7-: School of Foreign Languages, Beijing Institute of Technology.
- Teaching non-English major postgraduate students speaking, reading and writing courses, especially EAP courses now.
- Having published 13 papers and coauthored 16 books with colleagues.
- Having finished 3 projects with 2 still under research headed by me and taken part in 8 projects headed by others.
