## Basic Information
- Name: 姜丽蓉
- Pinyin: Jiang Lirong
- Title: 教授 / Professor
- School: 外国语学院 / The School of Foreign Language Studies
- Email: lrjiangbit@gmail.com

## Research Interests
- 翻译理论与实践 / Translation Theory and Practice
- 应用语言学 / Applied Linguistics
- 跨文化交际 / Inter-cultural Communication

## Biography
- 姜丽蓉，女，江苏扬州人。1982年毕业于山西大学外语系英语专业，获文学学士学位。曾就读于加拿大多伦多大学英语系。现任北京理工大学外国语学院教授，硕士生导师，拉各斯大学孔子学院中方院长。
- University Being Served: Beijing Institute of Technology
- Department: The School of Foreign Languages

## Education
- 1978-1982: Undergraduate student, Dept. of Foreign Languages, Shanxi University. Major: English Studies with French. Degree awarded: Bachelor of Art.
- 2004-2005: Visiting Scholar in English Department, University of Toronto, Canada.

## Work Experience
- 1982年: 由山西大学外语系毕业分配至太原机械学院基础部工作。
- 1993年: 任华北工学院外语部常务副主任（副处级）；被评为兵器工业总公司“优秀青年教师”。
- 1994年: 任外语部主任（正处级）；被评为山西省“三育人”先进个人。
- 1995年: 任华北工学院外语系主任（正处级）；获山西省“人文社会科学研究优秀成果”二等奖（排序第一）。
- 1996年: 晋升为教授。
- 1998年: 作为引进人才调入北京理工大学外语系，在外语学院工作至今。
- 1999年: 被评为北京理工大学“百名跨世纪优秀人才”。
- 2002年8月: 任硕士生导师。
- 2011年11月: 赴尼日利亚任拉各斯大学孔子学院中方院长。
- 2014年12月: 被评为全球孔子学院先进个人。
- 1996-present: Professor of English.
- 1991-1996: Associate professor of English; Head of Dept. of Foreign Languages, North China Institute of Technology.
- 1982-1998: Department of Foreign Languages, North China Institute of Technology.
- 1998-present: School of Foreign Languages, Beijing Institute of Technology.
- 2002: Supervisor of postgraduate.
- 2011-2015: Chinese Director of Confucius Institute at University of Lagos.

## Awards
- 1993: Excellence Youth Teacher by Educational Commission of Ordnance Industry.
- 1994: Excellence Teacher by Educational Commission of Shanxi Province, PR China.
- 1995: 2nd prize for Outstanding Achievements in Humane & Social Studies awarded by Educational Commission of Shanxi Province.
- 1999: One of A Hundred Outstanding Trans-century Talents by BIT.
- 2014: Confucius Institute Individual Performance Excellence Award.
