## Basic Information
- Name: 姜楠
- Pinyin: Jiang Nan
- Title: 副教授 / Associate Professor
- School: 外国语学院 / The School of Foreign Language Studies
- Email: jiangnanmx@163.com

## Research Interests
- 高等中文教育 / University Chinese Education
- 中国古典文学 / Classical Chinese Literature

## Education
- 1988-1992: 兰州大学中文系，文学学士 / Bachelor of Arts, Chinese Language and Literature, Lanzhou University.
- 1992-1995: 兰州大学中文系，文学硕士 / Master of Arts, Chinese Language and Literature, Lanzhou University.
- 2009至今: 南开大学文学院，博士在读 / 2009-Present, Ph.D. Candidate, College of Chinese Language and Culture at Nankai University.

## Work Experience
- 1995年至今: 北京理工大学任教 / 1995 to present: Faculty at Beijing Institute of Technology.
- Major Courses Taught: University Chinese Language and Literature; Introduction to Classics; Advanced Chinese Language and Literacy; Fundamental Chinese Language for International Students.
- 主要教授课程：大学语文；经典名著导读；语文高级素养；基础汉语（留学生）。

- 2011, Elective course “University Chinese Language and Literature” was awarded as Outstanding Course of the University.
