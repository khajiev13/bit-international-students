## Research Interests
Biomolecular & Metabolic Engineering, Synthetic Biology, Biotransformation (postdoc/Ph.D students positions available)

Research Program
Our research has focused on the biochemistry and regulation of microbial metabolism in engineered production strains. We use metabolic engineering, synthetic biology, and systems biology to construct microorganisms to produce bulk chemicals and value-added chemicals.

## Education
- B.Sc. 1999 Microbiology, Nankai University, P.R.China
- Ph.D. 2005 Molecular Microbiology & Virology, Université Paris 7-Denis Diderot, France
- Ph.D. 2005 Biochemistry and Molecular Biology, Peking University, P.R.China
(Exchange Ph.D. student, diploma awarded by two Universities separately)

- Postdoctoral Research Fellow, Department of Chemistry and Biochemistry, UCLA (2006-2008)
