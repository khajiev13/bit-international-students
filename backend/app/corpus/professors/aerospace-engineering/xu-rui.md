## Education
教育背景：（Educational Background）
Dr. Xu Rui, Associate Professor of the School of Aerospace Engineering at Beijing Institute of
Technology（BIT）， received his Ph.D from the Harbin Institute of Technology（HIT） in 2004. From 1994 to
2000 Dr. Xu studied autonomous control theory and flight vehicle design, and received his master and
bachelor's degree from HIT.
