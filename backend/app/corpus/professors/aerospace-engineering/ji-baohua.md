## Education
教育背景：（Educational Background）
Dr. Baohua Ji is current the professor in biomechanics and applied mechanics of Beijing Institute of
Technology. He got his B.S and Ph.D degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University of China in 1993 and 1998，
respectively. He did his postdoc research at Institute of Mechanics of Chinese Academy of Science during
1998-2001, and at Max-Planck Institute for Metal Research during 2001-2004.

## Work Experience
工作经历：（Working Experience）
In 2004, he joined the faculty of Department of Engineering Mechanics of Tsinghua University. In 2008, he
moved to the Department of Applied Mechanics of Beijing Institute of Technology.
