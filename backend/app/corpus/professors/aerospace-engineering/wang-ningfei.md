## Research Interests
研究方向（ResearchInterests）
1.Solid rocket motor design and rescarch
2.Combustion instability
3.Combustion and flow of Solid Fuel Scramjet
4.Electronic propulsion
5.Under water propulsion.
In 2004, he joined the faculty at BIT as the academic leader. His research interests cover a wide spectrum of
areas focusing on combustion, flow-field, energetic materials, and chemical propulsion, electric propulsion, etc.
He bas supervised 20 Ph.D and 25 M.S theses. He was the author or co-aLthor of more than 100 rescarch papers，
and has published one book on pressure coupled response of solid propellants.

## Education
教育背景：（Educational Background）
Wang Ningfei, Professor and Associate Dean of the School of Aerospace Engineering at Beijing Institute of
Technology, received his Ph.D from the Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2000.He received his master
and bachelor's degree from Beijing Institute of Technology in 1986 and 1983, respectively. Prior to that, he
worked in Xilan Modem Chemistry Research Institute from 1986-2004, and studied combustion mechanism in
University Duisburg as a researcher during 1989-1991.
