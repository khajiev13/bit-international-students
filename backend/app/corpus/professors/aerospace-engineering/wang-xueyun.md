## Research Interests
研究方向（Research Interests）
1.Feroic Domains 铁性畴结构
2.Scanning Probe Techniques 扫描探针技术
3.Single Crystal Growth 单晶生长
My research interests focus on the investigation of multifunctional ferroic domain and domain walls，
including their structures, distributions, 3-dimensional evolution and their physical properties, by
using both room temperature and cryogenic scanning probe technique.
