## Education
教育背景：（Educational Background）
Xia Qunli, associate professor and doctoral supervisot, graduated from major of missile launch of vehicle
engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology （BIT） and got his bachelor's degree in 1993. After that, he
received his master's degree and Ph.D at BIT in 1996 and 1999 from major of flight mechanics and flight
vehicle design of vehicle engineering,respectively. In the subsequent two years, he worked in post-doctoral
research station of armament science and technology at BIT.

## Work Experience
工作经历：（Working Experience）
Later, he became a teacher of BIT. After years' of hard work, he was promoted to associate professor in 2002
In recent years, associate professor XIA had taken charge of chief designer and vice director successively in
research of air force equipment model development. At the same time, he had a wide range of cooperation
with other aviation aerospace research organizations in areas such as reentry vehicle, control of maneuvering
reentry wathead, a new generation of air-to-air missile, air force intelligent patrol tly ammunition, etc. He
used to participate in the textbook compilation of two books and had published dozens of professional and
technical papers.
