## Research Interests
研究方向（Research Interests）
1.Mechanics of composite materials；
2.Micromechanics and metamaterial design；
3.Wave control；
4.Vibration control of large deployable space structures；
5.Particle motion in electric fields.
Dean of School of Science （BIT） during 2006-2008. His research interests cover a wide spectrum of areas
focusing on mechanics of composite materials, micromechanics, wave control and metamaterial design，
vibration control for large deployable space structutes.

## Education
教育背景：（Educational Background）
Professor Hu Gengkai received his Ph.D from the Ecole Centrale Paris （France） in 1991. He received his
bachelor's degree from Beijing Institute of Technology in 1986. He is visiting professor of Ecole Centrale de
Paris （France）， Ecole Nationale Superieure des Arts et Metiers （France）， Hongkong University of Science and
Technology, Cambridge University, Ecole Nationale Superieure de Cachan （France）.
