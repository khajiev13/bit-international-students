## Education
教育背景：（Educational Background）
Zhang Jingrui, professor of the School of Aerospace Engineering and Vice Director of Academic Aftairs
Office of the graduate school at Beijing Institute of Technology, obtained her automation instrument
bachelor' s degree and flight mechanics master's degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 1996 and
1998 respectively. She received her automaticcontrol Ph.D. degree from the Universite de Picardie -Jule
Veme in France in 2002.

## Work Experience
工作经历：（Working Experience）
After getting the Ph.D. diploma, she conducted her research work at post-doctoral research
station of
Tsinghua University from 2002 to 2004. In 2004, she was introduced into BIT as the senior personnel and
continued to carry out her teaching and research work. She set up the Distributed Spacecraft System Institute
of Technology in 2010.

## Academic Service and Memberships
学术兼职：（Part-time Academic Jobs）
Prof. Zhang is a part-time researcher of National Key Laboratory of Aerospace Dynamics, the 15th NPC
deputy of Haidian District and deputy director of China Democratic League Youth Committee of Haidian
District.
