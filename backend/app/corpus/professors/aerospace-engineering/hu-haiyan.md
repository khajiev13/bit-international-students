## Education
教育背景：（Educational Background）
1982年毕业于山东大学工程力学专业，获理学学士；1984年毕业于该校固体力学专业，获工学
硕士
1988年毕业于南京航空航天大学固体力学专业，获工学博士。
He received B. Sc. in engineering mechanics in 1982 and M. Sc. in solid mechanics in 1984 both from
Shandong University. Then he received Ph. D. in solid mechanics from Nanjing University ofi
Aeronautics and Astronautics in 1988.

## Work Experience
工作经历：（Working Experience）
1988年任南京航空航天大学飞行器工程系讲师，1990年任副教授，1994年任教授；2007年起
任北京理工大学宇航学院教授。
He joined Department of Aircraft Engineering, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics as
a Lecturer in 1988, became an Associate Professor in 1990 and a Professor in 1994. He has been
Protessor in School of Aerospace Engineering Beijing Institute of Technology since 2007.

## Academic Service and Memberships
学术兼职：（Part-time Academic Jobs）
2010年~2014年任中国力学学会理事长，2001 年~2014年任中国航空学会副理事长。现任国际
理论与应用力学联盟理事，中国宇航学会副理事长，ASME Joumal of Computational and Nonlincar
Dynamics 等3种国际期刊副主编和5种国际期刊编委。
He used to be President of Chinese Society of Theoretical and Applied Mechanics from 2010 to 2014
and Vice President of Chinese Society of Aeronautics from 2001 to 2014. He is currently General
Assembly Member of Intemational Union of Theoretical and Applied Mechanics, Vice President of
Chinese Society of Astronautics, Associate Editor for 3 intemational joumals, such as ASME Joumal
of Computational and Nonlinear Dynamics.
