- 1986-1988: Beijing Institute of Technology, M.S., Electromagnetic field and microwave technology.

## Work Experience
- 1999-Present time: Beijing Institute of Technology, Full Professor and Doctoral supervisor.
- 1994-1999: Beijing Institute of Technology, Associate Professor and Master supervisor.
- 1982-1994: Beijing Institute of Technology, Assistant Professor.

## Academic Service and Memberships
- Reviewer for Natural Science Foundation of China, several international conferences, and many international journals.

## Awards
